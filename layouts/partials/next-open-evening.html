Covid-19 Update: Build Brighton is once again accepting new members!

Open evenings have resumed, allowing you to visit the space, take a look around, and ask any questions you may have. If you're interested in joining, we can run you through the sign up process.

Open Evenings are usually held Thursdays 6-9pm, with some irregular weekend afternoon dates available on enquiry. You can arrange to attend one now if you would like to join.

There are currently spaces available, though there has been a high level of interest post-lockdown, so we'd advise attending an open evening and joining ASAP to secure a space. If you are interested in joining, or you have any questions, please e-mail info@buildbrighton.com.

The rule of 6 for indoor mixing is applied, with sanitiser freely provided in the space. Members use a booking system and personalised electronic keys to log entry to the space and ensure your sessions do not exceed 6 simulatenous users. At 1300 square feet, social distancing within the workshop is easily achievable.

Users must wear face masks and sanitise regularly while in the space, and practice social distancing between users of Build Brighton and other studios.

We look forward to meeting you!
